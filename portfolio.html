Our Growing Process

We follow a strategic approach to ensure every crop cycle delivers maximum flavor, nutrition, and environmental savings.

01

Seed Germination

We source certified non-GMO seeds and germinate them in organic coconut coir substrates under optimized moisture levels.

02

Vertical Growth Phase

Seedlings are transferred to vertical arrays where they absorb water-circulated organic minerals and target LED light spectrums.

03

Harvest & Delivery

Our team harvests the produce at its nutritional peak, packing it immediately for local door-to-door delivery within 24 hours.
